From Humble Beginnings

Robert's social media story is truly one of social media finding him instead of the other way around. While in the midst of rehabbing and learning to walk once again after a fall, he was sitting in a Michigan living room with one of his friends, when they decided to film a video of him walking and speaking about his journey and put in on TikTok. The rest is history!
